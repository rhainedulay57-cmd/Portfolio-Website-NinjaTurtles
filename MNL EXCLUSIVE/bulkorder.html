Bulk Orders

At MNL Exclusive, we offer special pricing and customized services for customers who wish to purchase clothing items in bulk. This Bulk Order Policy outlines the terms, process, and guidelines for all bulk transactions.

1. Definition of Bulk Orders

Bulk orders are transactions that meet any of the following conditions:

- Minimum of 20 pieces per design OR

- Minimum purchase amount of ₱30,000 - ₱40,000

- Custom print or custom-made apparel orders, regardless of quantity

3. Customization Options

For shirt printing, embroidery, or custom designs:

- Additional fees apply based on design size and complexity

- A digital mock-up will be provided before production

- Customer must approve the design before manufacturing begins

4. Order Process

1.Submit Inquiry

- Provide design details, quantity, sizes, and target date through our order form, Facebook page, or website.

2.Quotation

- We will send the full pricing breakdown, production time, and payment instructions.

3.Approval & Payment

- A 50% down payment is required before production starts. Balance must be settled before shipping or pickup.

4.Production

Standard production time:

- 7–14 business days (non-custom items)

- 10–21 business days (custom-printed/embroidered items)

5.Delivery/Shipping

- We ship via J&T, LBC, or other local couriers

- Shipping fee is shouldered by the buyer unless otherwise stated

- Free pickup option available

5. Payment Methods

We accept:

- GCash

- Bank Transfer (BDO/BPI)

- Cash (for pickups)

6. Return & Exchange Policy for Bulk Orders

To maintain quality and fairness:

- Bulk orders are non-refundable once production has started

- Exchanges are allowed only for items with factory defects

- Defective items must be reported within 3 days of receiving the order

- Size exchanges are not allowed for bulk orders, especially custom items

7. Order Cancellation

- If the customer cancels before production starts, the DP is refundable minus a 20% admin fee

- If production has already started, down payment is non-refundable

8. Rush Orders

Rush production is accepted case-by-case and may include:

- Additional rush fee (10–25%)

- Adjusted production time depending on our current workload

9. Shipping & Delivery Disclaimer

- We are not responsible for courier delays, lost parcels, or damages during shipping

- Tracking number will be provided once the order ships

- Customer must ensure the correct delivery address
