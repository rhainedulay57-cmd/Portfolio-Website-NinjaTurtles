Returns and Refunds
Policy

Definitions and legal references

The Merchant (referred to as "we", "us", or "our") operates the website www.mnlexclusive.com (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding returns and refunds.

  • The Service means the www.mnlexclusive.com website operated by MNL Exclusive.
  • Customer means the person who places an order through our Service.
  • Products means the clothing and fashion items available for purchase through our Service.

Our Exchange Policy

At MNL Exclusive, we want you to love your purchase! We accept exchanges within 7 days of delivery for the following reasons:

  • Defective items: Manufacturing defects or damage during shipping
  • Wrong item received: If you received a different product than what you ordered
  • Wrong size: If the size you received doesn't match your order

Exchange Requirements

To be eligible for an exchange, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Item must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached
  • Item must be in its original packaging
  • Request must be made within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) must be provided

Our Refund Policy

Important: Due to hygiene and the exclusive nature of our products, we do not accept returns for refunds on items that have been worn, washed, or used.

However, we will provide a full refund in the following cases:

  • Item arrived damaged or defective
  • Wrong item was shipped to you
  • Item is significantly different from the product description

All return requests for refunds must be made within 7 days of delivery.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Items that have been worn or used
  • Items that have been washed or altered
  • Items with tags removed or damaged packaging
  • Limited-edition or sale items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Intimate apparel or underwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items purchased more than 7 days ago

How to Request an Exchange or Return

To initiate an exchange or return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order via:
    • Email: mnlexclusive@gmail.com
    • TikTok: @primoromantiko
  2. Provide the following information:
    • Your order number
    • Photos of the item (showing defect or issue if applicable)
    • Reason for exchange or return
    • Preferred size or replacement item (for exchanges)
  3. Wait for approval: Our team will review your request within 1-2 business days
  4. Ship the item back: Once approved, we'll provide return shipping instructions

Shipping Costs

For Defective or Wrong Items

If the item is defective, damaged, or we shipped the wrong item, we will cover the return shipping costs. We will also cover the shipping cost for sending you the replacement item.

For Size Exchanges

For size exchanges on non-defective items, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. We will cover the shipping cost for sending you the new size.

Processing Time

Exchanges

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process your exchange within 2-3 business days. Your replacement item will be shipped within 1-2 business days after approval.

Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.
