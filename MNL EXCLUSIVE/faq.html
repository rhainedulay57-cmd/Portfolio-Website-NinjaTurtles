Frequently Asked Questions

Home

What is this website about? ▼
MNL Exclusive is your online hub for trendy, locally-inspired fashion. We focus on providing unique, high-quality clothing pieces for people who want to stand out while supporting Filipino designers.
How can I contact support? ▼
You can contact support via Email & Tiktok at mnlexclusive@gmail.com / @primoromantiko
How do I place an order? ▼
You can place an order by clicking the "Buy Now" button. And once you click it, there should be a choice on what clothes you wanted.
How long does the shipping take? ▼
The shipping takes at least 2-3 days to arrive!
What types of clothing are available in MNL Exclusive? ▼
We offer variety of items including streetwear, casual wear, and limited-edition fashion pieces designed for the modern Manila style.
Do you offer size options for all clothing items? ▼
Yes! Most items comes in sizes S, M, L, and XL. Some limited pieces may be one-size only, which will be indicated in the product description.
Are the materials used locally sourced? ▼
Yes, we prioritize using high-quality local fabrics whenever possible, supporting Filipino designers and textile producers.
Can I preorder limited-edition items? ▼
Yes! Certain exclusive releases allow pre-orders. Check the product page for availability and pre-order deadlines.
Do you ship outside Manila? ▼
Currently, shipping is limited to Metro Manila. Stay tuned for our updates as we expand our delivery options!
What is your return/exchange policy? ▼
We accept exchanges within 7 days of delivery for defective or wrong items. Unfortunately, due to hygiene and exclusivity, returns are not accepted for used or worn clothing.
